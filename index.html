About Me

I work as a Land Information Officer for Wood County Wisconsin. There I am responsible for coordinating the Land Records Modernization Plan and keeping the County's GIS data up to date. The work is full of challenges and keeps me pushing to learn new skills so that tremendous amount of information that is at my fingertips is more manageable.

Prior to my current position, I worked as a GIS Specialist for Lincoln County, Wisconsin. I have always had a passion for History, Cartography and I am glad that I am in a field where I get to experience a little bit of both.

I entered into the field of GIS because I liked maps and wanted to make them for a living. While I still do enjoy the cartography part of my work, I have gained a strong interest in programming. I continue to build my skills with every opportunity, as GIS is a constantly changing field and my skillset must continue to grow and adapt.
